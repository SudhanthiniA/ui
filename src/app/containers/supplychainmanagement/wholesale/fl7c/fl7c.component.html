"The permit-holder shall be bound to observe all rules incorporated from time to in the Excise Manual Volume I. Subject to the following special conditions which are in addition to the general conditions on all permit holders and licences under the provisions of the U.P. Excise Act, 1910 and the rules made thereunder, the infraction of any of which or conviction for any offence the U.P. Excise Act, 1910 or Narcotic Drugs and Psychotropic Substances Act,1985 shall render the permit liable to cancellation in addition to the forfeiture of the aforesaid security."

SPECIAL CONDITIONS
  1. Foreign liquor shall be supplied/ served by an approved person to the club, including their fide guests within promises detailed above and the same be consumed “ON” such premises only.
  2. The permit-holder shall maintain regular accounts in the prescribed register in Form F.L. 25 (or any other Form that may be prescribed by the Excise Commissioner from time to time)
  3. The permit-holder or his approved supplier/server shall produce the permit and Form F.L. 25 register immediately on demand by officers authorised to inspect the premises detailed above. The Form F.L.25 register shall always be kept on the above premises.
  4. Foreign liquor shall be supplied/served from noon to 11 p.m. on week days except Saturdays, and upto 12 p.m. on Saturdays.
  5. The permit-holder shall display the following warning at a prominent place on the above premises “Drinking is injurious to health”.
  6. Foreign liquor shall be supplied/served by standard pegs only. A standard peg shall be of 50 mls. a half peg shall be of 25 mls.
  7. The permit-holder shall not allow the foregin liquor supplied/served by him to be consumed “OFF” the premises detailed above.
  8. The permit-holder shall prevent drunkenness on the premises detailed above.
  9. The permit-holder shall immediately intimate to the Collector, any change in the constitution of the Club, or in the name of President or the Secretary.
